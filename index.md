# Privacy Policy for Allim
Effective date: March 6, 2026
Allim ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Allim mobile application (the "App").
Allim is named after the Korean word for "alarm," and helps you save stores, create reminders, and receive alerts when you are near places that matter to you.
## Information We Collect
Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:
- **Location Data** (precise or approximate): used to trigger nearby store reminders.
- **Reminder and Store Data**: such as saved stores, reminder titles, notes, and related list content.
- **Device/Notification Data**: used to deliver reminder notifications.
- **Basic Diagnostics** (if enabled): crash logs and performance data to improve app reliability.
## How We Use Information
We use data to:
- Provide core app features (saved stores, reminders, nearby alerts)
- Notify you when you are near selected stores
- Sync and support shared/collaborative grocery workflows (if applicable)
- Maintain app security, fix bugs, and improve performance
## Location Permissions
Allim may request location access to enable location-based reminders.  
You can control or revoke location permissions anytime in your device settings.
If background location access is granted, Allim can alert you when you are near saved stores even when the app is not open.
## Notifications
Allim may request permission to send notifications for reminders and nearby alerts.  
You can disable notifications at any time in device settings.
## Data Sharing
We do **not** sell your personal data.
We may share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate the App (for example, cloud hosting, analytics, or crash reporting), subject to appropriate safeguards.
## Data Retention
We keep data only as long as needed to provide the App and fulfill legitimate business or legal purposes.  
You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
## Children's Privacy
Allim is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country).  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
## Your Choices
You can:
- Review and edit your reminders/store data in the App
- Disable location access in device settings
- Disable notifications in device settings
- Request data deletion by contacting us
## Third-Party Services
If Allim uses third-party services (such as analytics, maps, cloud storage, or crash reporting), those providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.
